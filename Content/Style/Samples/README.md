# Writing Samples
*Your best writing goes here. The AI analyzes these to generate your writing style guide.*

## What to Put Here

Drop **5–10 pieces of your best writing** as markdown files. These should be pieces that sound most like *you* — the writing you're proudest of and want the AI to emulate.

**Good samples:**
- LinkedIn posts that got strong engagement
- Newsletter issues you're proud of
- Blog posts or articles in your natural voice
- Emails or messages where you nailed the tone
- Twitter/X threads that felt right

**File format:** Markdown (`.md`). One piece per file. Name them however you want — the content matters, not the filename.

**Quality over quantity.** 5 great samples beat 50 mediocre ones. The AI extracts patterns from your best work, so only include writing that represents the voice you want to reproduce.

## What Happens

The **style-analyzer** skill reads all samples in this folder and generates `Content/Style/writing-style.md` — a comprehensive style guide that any AI can use to write in your voice. It runs weekly and picks up new samples automatically, so your style guide evolves as your writing does.

You can also trigger it manually: say **"update my writing style"**.
